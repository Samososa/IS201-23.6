DISCLAIMER: This split has been personaly developed and will work DIFFERENTLY for everyone.

  1. Monday
    • Bench Press: Build to 90% or 100% of MAX then 3 x 8
    • Incline Bench Press 3 x 10
    • Chest Cable Fly 4 x 10
    • V-bar Tricep Push Downs 3 x 14
    • V-bar Tricep Push Downs 1 x Failure
    • Overhead Cable Tricep Extensions 4 x 14
    • Cable Shoulder Raises 4 x 10
    • Lateral Raises 3 x 12
  2. Tuesday
    • Deadlift: Build to 90% or 100% of MAX
    • Cable Rows Closegrip 4 x 14
    • Cable Pull Downs 4 x 10
    • Reardelt Flys Single Arm 4 x 12
    • Lat Pulldowns 3 x 12
    • Lat Pulldowns Reversegrip Closegrip 3 x 14
    • Bicep Curls 3 x 12
    • Eazy Bar Curls Reversegrip Closegrip 3 x Failure
  3. Wednesday
    • Bar Squats: Build Up to Max then 3 x 10
    • Seated Calf Raises 3 x 12
    • Romainian Deadlifts 3 x 12
    • Leg Curls 3 x 10
    • Leg Extensions 3 x 12
    • Leg Extensions 1 x Failure
  4. Thursday
    • Bench Press: Build to 90% or 100% of MAX then 3 x 5
    • Cable Rows Closegrip 4 x 10
    • Incline Bench Press 4 x 8
    • Lat Pulldowns 4 x 10
    • Chest Cable Fly 3 x 14
    • Cable Pull Downs 3 x 12
  5. Friday
    • Bicep Curls 4 x 12
    • V-bar Tricep Push Downs 3 x 14
    • V-bar Tricep Push Downs 1 x Failure
    • Eazy Bar Curls Widegrip 3 x Failure
    • Eazy Bar Curls Reversegrip Closegrip 3 x Failure
    • Overhead Cable Tricep Extensions 4 x Failure
    • Cable Shoulder Raises 4 x 12
    • Lateral Raises 3 x 10
    • Shoulder Press 4 x 10
  6. Saturday
    • Hack Squats: Warm up then 5 x 10
    • Seated Calf Raises 3 x 12
    • Romainian Deadlifts 3 x 12
    • Leg Curls 3 x 10
    • Leg Extensions 3 x 12
    • Leg Extensions 1 x Failure
    7. Back to Top
